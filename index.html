Who We Are

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an international association for technical professionals.

Networking

We understand the importance of the success of our students. We invite companies out to allow fellow alumni to speak about their experiences in the industry and what it took for them to get there.

Workshops

Through these, students become better equipped for the current jobs in the market. We aim to prepare them with the skills that make them more competitive candidates.

Competitions

Our biggest competition, IEEEXtreme, is a 24-hour online coding competition where students compete in teams to complete unique programming challenges. The Grand Prize is a trip to the IEEE conference of your choice, anywhere in the world.

Join

Interested in becoming a member? Here's the process:

  • STEP 1:

    Join IEEE

    Click this link to submit an application to join IEEE. Then, click the "Join as a student" button and follow the instructions to create an IEEE account and purchase a membership. Select “University of Houston - NSM College” during the registration process.

  • STEP 2:

    Find your member number

    On the "My Account" page, under "Personalize your IEEE experience", click "Personal Profile".

    Under "Personal information" on the "My Account Page", you should see "Member/Customer Number". Copy this number into the box below.

  • STEP 3:

    Join IEEE-NSM

    Click here to fill out our student membership form with your t-shirt size!
